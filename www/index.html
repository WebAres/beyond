WHAT ARE WE?

BEYOND “IT” IS A GLOBAL PLATFORM OF SUPPORT FOR PEOPLE AND INITIATIVES TO INVITE OUR CULTURE TO MOVE BEYOND THE LABELS & STEREOTYPES THAT LIMIT US FROM REACHING OUR FULL POTENTIAL.

OUR MISSION

IT IS THE MISSION OF BEYOND “it” TO USE DESIGN, PRODUCTS, AND PARTNERSHIPS TO GENERATE SUPPORT AND AWARENESS FOR INITIATIVES THAT MOVE THE WORLD BEYOND “it”.

HOW IT WORKS?

BEYOND “it” products fuel a philanthropic platform, powered by the people, to raise money for BEYOND “it” partnered initiatives.
